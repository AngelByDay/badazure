Travel Adventures are getting ready to launch their new website which is going to be hosted on the Microsoft Azure platform.

The team at TA are keen to have a really professional-looking web presence, but they're on a budget so they've decided to use Storage Accounts with Static Content to keep costs down.

Visit https://azuretrainer1.z13.web.core.windows.net/level1/ and have a look at the site, can you find any security misconfigurations?

Some of the assets on the page are being loaded from a different location to the page, have a look and see if the path for those assets gives you an indication of where to go next.
